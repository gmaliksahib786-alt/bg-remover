Frequently asked questions

Is this really free and unlimited?

Yes, 100% free and unlimited. The AI model runs in your browser — no server to pay for. No account, no credit card, no limits ever.

What is WebGPU and why does it matter?

WebGPU is a modern browser API that uses your GPU for AI inference. On supported browsers (Chrome 113+), processing takes ~0.5 seconds even for 4K images. On other browsers, WASM is used automatically (2-5 seconds).

Why does first use take time?

First time, RMBG-1.4 quantized model (~45MB) downloads to your browser cache. After that it's instant — even works offline.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No. Everything runs locally in a Web Worker in your browser. Images never leave your device — no uploads, no cloud, no tracking.

Which browsers support WebGPU?

Chrome 113+, Edge 113+, and Opera 99+ support WebGPU. Safari has partial support. Firefox doesn't yet. On unsupported browsers, the tool automatically uses WASM — still works great.
